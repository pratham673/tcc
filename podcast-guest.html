We host one episode per month with cloud operators, MSP leaders, and IT decision-makers who have a real story to share. No vendor pitches. No marketing speak. Just honest conversations about what's working in cloud operations today.

Who we want to talk to

Who's the right guest

MSP owners and leaders

Especially those running practices between 5 and 50 staff, working through real growth challenges or operational shifts.

FinOps & cloud cost practitioners

People doing the actual work of reducing cloud spend at scale, not just theorizing about it.

IT and security leaders

CIOs, CTOs, Heads of IT, and security leaders with strong points of view on where cloud operations are heading.

What to expect

How the podcast works

Four steps from first conversation to launch day.

01

Initial conversation

A 20-minute call to align on topic, story angle, and what makes your perspective valuable to the audience.

02

Recording session

45 to 60 minutes recorded over Riverside or Zencastr. Professional audio quality. No technical setup needed on your end.

03

Editing and production

We handle all editing, show notes, transcripts, and cover art. You get a final version to approve before publishing.

04

Distribution and amplification

Episode goes live on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube, and via the newsletter. We promote across LinkedIn. You amplify to your network.
