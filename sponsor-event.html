Event types

Where you can sponsor

Virtual roundtables

60 minutes. Recorded. Highlights shared post-event.

In-person gatherings in Melbourne, Sydney, and select US cities. 30 to 80 attendees. Networking-focused. Strong for local brand presence.

Sponsorship tiers

How sponsorship works

We keep tiers simple. Three options. Clear value at each level.

Tier 01

Supporting sponsor

Per eventIndicative range $2,500 to $5,000
  • Logo placement on all event materials
  • Mention in pre-event and post-event communications
  • Dedicated thank-you in event recording
  • One designated representative attendance
Tier 02 · Most popular

Headline sponsor

Per eventIndicative range $7,500 to $15,000
  • All Supporting Sponsor benefits
  • Branded segment in the event itself (5 to 10 minute featured slot)
  • Co-promotion across newsletter and LinkedIn
  • Attendee list access (opt-in only, GDPR compliant)
  • Two designated representative attendances

Annual partner

Per yearCustom packages from $25,000
  • Headline Sponsor benefits across multiple events
  • Quarterly content collaboration
